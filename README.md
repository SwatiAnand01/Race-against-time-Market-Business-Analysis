# Race Against Time: The 10-Minute Delivery Revolution in India

## 📌 Overview
This project presents a comprehensive strategic analysis of India's rapidly growing quick-commerce industry. It compares four leading players—Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, and BigBasket—to understand how they compete in the race for ultra-fast deliveries.

The analysis explores market trends, business models, operational strategies, financial performance, consumer expectations, and future opportunities within the 10-minute delivery ecosystem.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze India's quick-commerce landscape.
- Compare major industry players across multiple business dimensions.
- Evaluate operational and logistical challenges.
- Study marketing, funding, and growth strategies.
- Understand the future of quick commerce in India.
